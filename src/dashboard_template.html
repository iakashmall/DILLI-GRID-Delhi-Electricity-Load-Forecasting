02 · Model Arena

Seven models. Four stress conditions. One survivor.

Every candidate was scored not just on average accuracy, but on the hours that hurt: the top-5% peak load hours, 40 °C+ heatwave hours, and statistically abnormal days. Plain gradient-boosted trees crumble on record peaks (trees can't extrapolate) — predicting load relative to its recent level fixes that.

The Pipeline
STEP 1 · DATADelhi SLDC 15-min load (2010–13) + hourly Safdarjung weather, cleaned & merged to 25,867 hourly rows.
STEP 2 · MODELS7 models benchmarked chronologically on one unseen year, incl. peak / heatwave / outlier-day subsets.
STEP 3 · EXPORTWinning approach retrained on calendar+weather and exported as JSON trees for the browser.
STEP 4 · CONSOLEThis page — a single self-contained HTML file. Also servable via the included FastAPI app.
